Pneumonia Detection Web App

Pneumonia detection is a crucial step in identifying and treating this serious respiratory infection. This web application aims to assist in the early detection of pneumonia by analyzing chest X-ray images. Simply upload an image and our model will provide a prediction of whether or not pneumonia is present. Additionally, we provide helpful precautionary tips to help prevent the spread of respiratory illnesses such as pneumonia.

  • Avoid touching your face, especially your eyes, nose, and mouth.
  • Stay home if you feel unwell, and seek medical attention if you have a fever, cough, or difficulty breathing.
